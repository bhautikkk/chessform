Refund & Cancellation Policy

This policy outlines the conditions under which Chess Bird will process refunds for tournament registration fees on our website (available at https://chessbird.online), and the process for submitting a refund request. Please read this carefully before registering.

01

Policy Overview

Chess Bird charges a tournament registration fee to cover the costs of platform infrastructure, match organisation, opponent scheduling, and prize pool management. This fee is generally non-refundable once a tournament spot has been confirmed.

However, we recognise that exceptional circumstances can arise - including tournament cancellations by the organiser or accidental duplicate payments - and we have clearly defined the circumstances under which a refund will be granted.

Our Commitment: Where a refund is due under this policy, Chess Bird will process it promptly and transparently. We believe in fair dealing with all our participants and are committed to resolving payment concerns with integrity.

All refund requests must be submitted via one of the official support channels listed in Section 8 of this policy. Verbal requests or requests via unofficial channels will not be processed.

02

Situations Eligible for a Refund

A full refund of the registration fee will be issued in the following circumstances:

Refund Eligible
  • Tournament cancelled by Chess Bird before it begins
  • Duplicate payment for the same tournament slot
  • Payment debited but registration not confirmed (technical error)
  • Player registered but found ineligible due to rating miscommunication on our part
  • Tournament permanently cancelled after partial completion (partial refund may apply)
Not Eligible
  • Voluntary withdrawal after registration is confirmed
  • Disqualification due to cheating or fair play violations
  • Failure to appear for scheduled matches (no-show)
  • Registration with incorrect personal details provided by the player
  • Change of personal circumstances or schedule conflicts
03

Duplicate Payment Refund Policy

If you have accidentally submitted two or more payments for the same tournament registration, Chess Bird will refund the duplicate amount(s) in full.

  • A duplicate payment is defined as more than one successful payment transaction made by the same user for the same tournament, resulting in a single confirmed registration slot.
  • If you believe you have made a duplicate payment, you must contact us within 7 days of the payment date with your transaction reference number(s) and the registered email address.
  • Duplicate payment refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days of verification and returned to the original payment method.
  • If the duplicate payment resulted in two separate tournament registrations being confirmed (e.g., registering two players), this will not be treated as a duplicate and the standard refund terms apply.

How to Report: Contact our support team with both transaction reference IDs, your registered email address, and a brief description of the duplicate payment. We will cross-verify with our payment records and initiate the refund within 2 business days of confirmation.

04

Non-Refundable Situations

The following situations are not eligible for a refund under any circumstances. Please review this list carefully before registering and making payment.

  • Voluntary Withdrawal: If you choose to withdraw from a tournament after your registration has been confirmed and your payment processed, you are not entitled to a refund, regardless of the reason.
  • No-Show Without Prior Notice: If you fail to appear for your scheduled match(es) without notifying the administrator in advance, your registration fee is forfeited.
  • Disqualification for Fair Play Violations: Any participant disqualified for using chess engines, playing on another person's account, or any other fair play violation will not receive a refund under any circumstances.
  • Ineligibility Due to Player Error: If your registration is invalidated because you provided incorrect or fraudulent information (name, chess username, rating, etc.), no refund will be issued.
  • Fraudulent UTR Reuse: Submitting multiple registrations using a single transaction UTR is strictly prohibited. Only the first registration will be approved. Any subsequent registrations attempting to reuse the same UTR will be immediately rejected and are not eligible for any refund.
  • Platform or Internet Issues on the Player's Side: Technical difficulties on your device, ISP, or internet connection that result in missed matches are not grounds for a refund.
  • Change of Mind: Registration fees are non-refundable simply because you have changed your mind or your circumstances have changed after confirmed registration.
  • Partial Tournament Completion: If you have successfully played one or more rounds in a tournament before withdrawing or being eliminated through fair competition, no refund will be issued.

Important: By completing payment and confirming your registration, you acknowledge and accept that the registration fee is non-refundable except in the specific eligible circumstances described in Section 2 and Section 3 of this policy.

05

Refund Request Process

To submit a refund request, please follow these steps carefully. Requests that do not include the required information cannot be processed:

1
Gather Your Payment Details
Locate your UTR/transaction reference ID and the email address used for registration.
2
Contact Our Support Team
Reach out via email to chessbirdofficial@gmail.com or via WhatsApp/Telegram (details in Section 8). Use the subject line: "Refund Request - [Your Name] - [Tournament Name]".
3
Provide Required Information
Include your full name, registered email address, UTR/transaction ID, tournament name, amount paid, and a clear explanation of the reason for your refund request.
4
Verification by Our Team
Our team will verify your request against our payment and registration records. We may contact you for additional information or documentation.
5
Refund Initiation & Confirmation
If your request is approved, we will initiate the refund to your original payment method. You will receive a confirmation email with the refund reference number.

Please ensure that your refund request is submitted within 14 days of the event that gives rise to the refund claim (e.g., tournament cancellation date or duplicate payment date). Requests submitted after this period may not be honoured.

Refund timelines for credit/debit card transactions depend on the processing timelines of your issuing bank, which are beyond our control.

07

Failed Payment Reversals

If a payment is initiated but fails partway through (e.g., network interruption, bank gateway error) and the amount is debited from your account but the registration is not confirmed, the following applies:

  • In most cases, failed payment amounts are automatically reversed by your bank and credited back to your source account within 5-7 business days.
  • This automatic reversal is handled by your bank without any action required from Chess Bird.
  • If you do not receive the reversal within 7 business days, you should first contact your bank with the transaction reference number (UTR).
  • If the bank confirms that no reversal was received and the issue persists, contact Chess Bird support with your transaction details.
  • In all failed-payment cases where a debit occurred without confirmed registration, Chess Bird will cooperate fully to facilitate the reversal.

Quick Tip: After any payment, always check your email for a Chess Bird registration confirmation. If your account is debited but you don't receive the registration confirmation email, contact our support team immediately to investigate.
