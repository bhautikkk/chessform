Chess Bird is an online platform dedicated to organising and managing skill-based competitive chess tournaments. The platform provides registered participants with structured tournament brackets, opponent matching, match scheduling, leaderboard tracking, and prize pool management.

Skill-Based Competition — Not Gambling: Chess is a game of intellectual skill, strategy, and calculation. Chess Bird operates exclusively as a competitive sports platform. We do not engage in, facilitate, or promote gambling, betting, wagering, or any game of chance. The outcome of every match on this platform is determined entirely by the skill of the participants. Registration fees are charged to access platform infrastructure, not to place bets.

This platform is operated in India and is compliant with applicable Indian laws governing skill-based gaming competitions.

Fair Play Policy

Chess Bird is committed to maintaining the integrity and competitive fairness of all tournaments. All registered participants are required to adhere to the following fair play standards:

  • You must play using only the chess account registered at the time of sign-up. Playing on behalf of another person is strictly prohibited.
  • You must not consult any chess engine, computer assistance, database, or analytical tool during live match play.
  • You must not seek or receive move suggestions from any other person (whether online or in person) during your active matches.
  • You must not deliberately lose matches (sandbagging) or engage in match-fixing of any kind.
  • You are expected to maintain respectful and sportsmanlike conduct toward all opponents, administrators, and other participants.
  • Any form of collusion between players to manipulate tournament standings or prize distribution is strictly prohibited.

Participants are expected to comply with the fair play rules of the relevant chess platform (Chess.com or Lichess) in addition to these terms.

Anti-Cheating Policy

Chess Bird operates a zero-tolerance policy against any form of cheating. We employ multiple methods to detect and investigate suspected violations:

  • All games played during our tournaments are subject to post-game analysis by our anti-cheating team using recognised chess analysis methodologies.
  • We monitor game data for statistically anomalous move accuracy, engine correlation, and abnormal time usage patterns.
  • Any player whose account is flagged or banned by Chess.com or Lichess for fair play violations during an active tournament period will be immediately disqualified.

Identity Verification

  • Chess Bird reserves the right to require any participant to undergo a live online video verification call at any stage of the tournament.
  • During such a call, you may be asked to log in to your registered chess account on camera to confirm that you are the actual account holder.
  • Refusal to participate in a requested verification call will be treated as an admission of account fraud and will result in immediate disqualification and a permanent ban.

Consequences of Cheating: Any player found to have cheated, used engine assistance, played on another player's account, or engaged in any other form of fraud will be: (1) immediately disqualified from the current tournament, (2) permanently banned from all future Chess Bird events, and (3) will forfeit their entire prize entitlement. The registration fee will not be refunded.

Match Scheduling Rules

Match schedules are established and communicated by the tournament organisers. All participants are expected to strictly adhere to the following scheduling rules:

  • Match schedules will be communicated via our official Telegram group and/or email. It is your responsibility to monitor these channels.
  • You must be online and ready on the designated chess platform at the scheduled match time. A grace period of 10 minutes will be allowed before a walkover is awarded to the waiting player.
  • If you require a schedule change, you must notify the tournament administrator through our support channels at least 24 hours before the scheduled match time. Reschedules are subject to administrator approval and opponent availability, and are not guaranteed.
  • Players who repeatedly fail to appear for scheduled matches may be removed from the tournament at the administrator's discretion.
  • In the event of technical difficulties (internet outage, platform downtime), you must notify the administrator immediately via WhatsApp or Telegram. The administrator will assess the situation and determine an appropriate course of action, including rescheduling if warranted.
