Academy Overview & Nature of Service

Chess Bird Academy is an online educational platform dedicated to organising and managing chess coaching, training sessions, and student evaluations. The platform provides registered students with structured training modules, peer practice sessions, batch scheduling, performance progress tracking, and academic evaluation rewards.

Educational Platform — Not a Gaming Service: Chess is a game of intellectual skill, strategy, and calculation. Chess Bird Academy operates exclusively as a chess educational coaching portal. We do not engage in, facilitate, or promote gambling, betting, wagering, or real-money gaming. The outcomes of practice games are evaluated entirely for training and student grading purposes. Enrollment fees are charged to access academy coaching services and training materials.

This platform is operated in India and is compliant with applicable Indian laws governing educational training and coaching services.

Eligibility & Enrollment Requirements

To enroll in Chess Bird Academy programs, you must meet all of the following eligibility criteria:

  • You must possess a valid, active, and publicly accessible account on Chess.com and/or Lichess.org as specified in the program announcement.
  • Your chess account must not be flagged, banned, or restricted by Chess.com or Lichess for fair play violations at the time of enrollment.
  • You must provide your correct full name, email address, phone number, and chess username during registration. Providing false information will result in immediate dismissal from the academy without refund.
  • Each student may enroll in a specific training batch only once. Duplicate registrations are not permitted and any additional payments made in error will be refunded per our Refund Policy.
  • Participation in specific learning tiers may be restricted based on rating categories or previous training accomplishments as specified in individual batch descriptions.

05

Fair Learning Policy

Chess Bird Academy is committed to maintaining the educational integrity of all coaching programs. All enrolled students are required to adhere to the following learning standards:

  • You must participate using only the chess account registered at the time of sign-up. Playing practice games on behalf of another student is strictly prohibited.
  • You must not consult any chess engine, computer assistance, database, or analytical tool during live training games or evaluations.
  • You must not seek or receive move suggestions from any other person (whether online or in person) during active training sessions.
  • You must play in the spirit of learning and not deliberately throw games or engage in manipulation of evaluation scores.
  • You are expected to maintain respectful, polite, and sportsmanlike conduct toward all coaches, administrators, and fellow students.
  • Any form of collusion between students to manipulate training rankings is strictly prohibited.

Students are expected to comply with the community guidelines and fair play rules of the relevant chess platform (Chess.com or Lichess) in addition to these terms.

06

Academic Integrity Policy

Chess Bird Academy operates a zero-tolerance policy against any form of academic dishonesty or unfair assistance. We employ multiple methods to detect and investigate suspected violations during evaluations:

  • All games played during our coaching sessions are subject to post-game analysis by our coaching team using recognised chess analysis tools.
  • We monitor game data for statistically anomalous move accuracy and engine correlation during student assessments.
  • Any student whose account is flagged or banned by Chess.com or Lichess for fair play violations during their academy tenure will be immediately dismissed.

Identity Verification

  • Chess Bird Academy reserves the right to require any student to undergo a live online video verification call during evaluations.
  • During such a call, you may be asked to log in to your registered chess account on camera to confirm that you are the student completing the exercises.
  • Refusal to participate in a requested verification call will be treated as a violation of academic integrity and will result in immediate dismissal and a permanent ban from the academy.

Consequences of Dishonesty: Any student found to have cheated, used engine assistance, shared accounts, or engaged in other forms of dishonesty will be: (1) immediately dismissed from the training course, (2) permanently banned from all future Chess Bird Academy sessions, and (3) will forfeit their entire scholarship or certificate entitlement. The enrollment fee will not be refunded.

Session Scheduling Rules

Class and practice session schedules are established by the academy coordinators. All students are expected to strictly adhere to the scheduling rules:

  • Batch schedules and lesson timings will be communicated via our official Telegram channel and/or email. It is your responsibility to monitor these updates.
  • You must be online and ready on the designated chess platform at the scheduled training time. A grace period of 10 minutes will be allowed before a student is marked absent for that session.
  • If you require a schedule change or need to request a makeup session, you must notify the academy coordinator at least 24 hours before the scheduled class time. Reschedules are subject to batch availability and coordinator approval.
  • Students who repeatedly fail to appear for scheduled training sessions may be removed from the batch at the coordinator's discretion.
  • In the event of technical difficulties (internet outage, platform downtime), you must notify the coordinator immediately via WhatsApp or Telegram so that a backup lesson can be planned.
