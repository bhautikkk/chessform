Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register for Chess Bird tournaments, we collect the following information:

  • Full Name
  • Chess.com Username
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Chess Rating (fetched automatically)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To register you for our exclusive chess tournaments.
  • To verify your identity and chess rating to ensure fair play.
  • To communicate tournament schedules, updates, and prize distributions.
  • To maintain the integrity of our leaderboard and ranking system.

3. Data Protection and Security

Your privacy is our top priority. All personal and payment information is securely transmitted and stored using industry-standard encryption protocols. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services like Razorpay for payment processing and Firebase for secure database management. These services have their own stringent privacy policies and security measures.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our official Telegram channel.
